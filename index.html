FAQ

Admission Related Questions
Parents Ask

Find answers to common questions about admissions, curriculum, and campus life at KVGIT.

Is B.Com from KVGIT valid for government jobs and higher studies?

+

Yes. KVGIT is affiliated to University of Rajasthan (UOR) and approved by DCE, Govt. of Rajasthan. A B.Com degree from KVGIT is fully valid for government job applications, M.Com admissions, CA/CS pursuits, and corporate careers across India.

What is the admission process for B.Com at KVGIT?

+

No entrance exam is required. Admission is based on 10+2 marks and eligibility criteria. Students simply fill the enquiry form and the admissions team guides them through the complete process step by step.

Can a student from Science or Arts background join B.Com?

+

Yes. B.Com is open to students from any stream who have passed 10+2 with at least 48% aggregate marks from a recognised board.

What is the total fee for the B.Com programme?

+

The total fee for the 3-year B.Com programme is ₹90,150 (₹30,050 per year) without scholarships or discounts. Government and SKVE Trust scholarships can significantly reduce this amount for eligible students.

What career options are available after B.Com from KVGIT?

+

B.Com graduates from KVGIT can pursue careers as Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Finance Officers, Investment Consultants, Sales Managers, Financial & Insurance Consultants, and Government sector roles in Banking, Railway, and Finance.
